Frequently Asked Questions

For Buyers

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the 'Track Order' section in your account and entering your order ID.

How do I return an item?

You can initiate a return by going to the 'Order History' section and selecting the 'Return' option for your order.

For Vendors

How do I list products?

Log into your vendor account, and under 'Product Management', click on 'Add New Product' to start listing items.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are directly transferred to your linked bank account within 5-7 business days after the sale is completed.
