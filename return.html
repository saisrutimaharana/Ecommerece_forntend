Return & Refund Policy

Our return and refund policy is designed to ensure a seamless and transparent process for our customers.

Return Process

To initiate a return, visit the 'Order History' section of your account and select the item you wish to return. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the return process.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you via email. Your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Conditions for Return

  • Items must be in their original condition with all tags intact.
  • Return requests must be submitted within 30 days of receiving the order.
  • Certain items like personalized goods and perishable products cannot be returned.
